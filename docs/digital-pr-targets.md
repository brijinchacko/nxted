# Digital-PR target list + pitch templates (operator)

AI answer engines quote third-party "best providers" roundups and reputable
newsletters directly, so a mention in those is worth more than most on-page work.
Outreach is your job; this is the target list + ready pitches. **Be honest and
specific - no inflated claims.**

## Target types (work these in order)

1. **"Best data providers / labelling companies" roundups** that already rank for
   the buyer keywords. Search these queries and note who publishes the listicles:
   - "best egocentric data providers for robotics"
   - "best robotics training data companies"
   - "Scale AI alternatives" / "Appen alternatives"
   - "RLHF data providers"
   Vendors and blogs (e.g. Labellerr, Unidata, Encord, Kili, and similar) publish
   these roundups; ask to be added with a one-paragraph, accurate description.

2. **Robotics / physical-AI newsletters & blogs** (e.g. The Robot Report, robotics
   substacks, embodied-AI newsletters). Offer a data-angle guest post or a quote.

3. **Hugging Face / open-source robotics community** - publishing the sample
   dataset (see huggingface-dataset-card.md) earns a citation-grade backlink.

4. **HARO-style / journalist request services** - respond to queries about
   robotics data, the EU AI Act for AI data, or India's role in AI supply chains.

5. **Podcasts / panels** on physical AI, robotics data, or AI compliance.

## Pitch template A - listicle inclusion

> Subject: nxted for your "{{egocentric data providers / robotics data}}" roundup
>
> Hi {{name}}, I run nxted (OFORO LTD, UK). We supply egocentric physical-AI
> training data and expert RLHF evaluation from India - consented, robotics-ready
> (LeRobot/RLDS/HDF5) and UK/EU-contracted, with a Data Trust Pack for compliance.
> Our wedge is skilled **industrial** work (electrical assembly, CNC, electronics)
> that open datasets and gig collectors don't cover. If you're updating
> {{article}}, we'd be glad to provide an accurate one-paragraph description and a
> logo. Happy to answer anything. - {{founder}}

## Pitch template B - guest post / quote

> Subject: Guest post: "{{Why skilled-trade demonstrations beat generic factory footage}}"
>
> Hi {{name}}, I'd like to contribute an original, sourced piece for {{outlet}} on
> {{topic}}. It cites primary research (Open X-Embodiment, the ICLR 2025
> imitation-learning scaling-laws paper, EU AI Act) and avoids vendor fluff.
> Outline attached; no payment or links required beyond a short author bio. We can
> also provide a quote for any robotics-data or AI-Act piece you're writing.

## Tracking

- Keep a sheet of: target, type, contact, date, status, outcome (link/mention).
- Re-run the 30-query baseline (docs/seo-geo-measurement.md) monthly; watch
  whether new third-party mentions correlate with more AI-engine citations.
